Services

Cartoon Design

An animator needs to understand how the body moves and to be aware of the 12 principles of animation. Technical animator skills include audio/video editing and cutting-edge softwares like Adobe Flash, 3D Studio Max, and more

Graphic Design Skills

The main responsibility of an animator is to bring elements to life and for that it needs to have knowledge of graphic motion. Animators should know how to create figures, visual effects, and how to make animations more real to live.

Color Theory

Know how to use colors on your characters and what color you should use to express different emotions, feelings, etc. You have to think at what your animation want to communicate to the audience and to choose the perfect colors for that.

Sense of timing

An animator needs to use their knowledge of animation and to be able to move elements in order to create natural and soft movements. Also, he has to have a good sense of timing and spacing, to anticipate movements, to action and to react.

Originality

It may takes years to develop your own style, but every animator should make their mark and create original animations that people will remember with pleasure. Make your art unique and so you make yourself known in this industry.

Analyzing skills

A good animator must pay attention at small details. These details, like where is the character looking, how fast the figure should move, an eye-blink, etc. make the difference and could give your animation a more professional look.
